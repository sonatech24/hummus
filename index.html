Hummus

Ingredients


  • â…“ cup tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • fresh lemone juice
  • pinch of salt
  • fresh parsley
  • 3 cups hummus

Method


  • Step 1

    Take 1 cup dry hummus and place it in a large bowl. Add plenty of water and soak overnight (hummus will expand in the bowl).When ready, drain hummus and place them in a medium-sized heavy cooking pot. Cover with water by about 2 inches. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 to 2 hours.

  • Step 2

    Cover the cooked hummus in hot water and add 1 teaspoon baking soda. Leave for a few minutes. Take a handful of hummus and rub under running water to remove the skins.. Let the hummus cool completely before using.

  • Step 3

    Dry the hummus well then add them to the bowl of a large food processor that's fitted with a blade. Run the processor until the hummus turn into a smooth powder-like paste

  • Step 4

    While the food processor is running, add 2 ice cubes, tahini, salt, and lemon juice. Blend for about 4 minutes or so. Check, and if the consistency is too thick still, run processor and slowly add a little hot water. Blend until you reach desired silky smooth consistency. At this point, you can cover and refrigerate the hummus for an hour or so before serving.

  • Step 5

    Spread in a serving bowl and add a generous drizzle of good extra virgin olive oil. Add a few hummus to the middle, if you like. Sprinkle sumac on top , then serve immediately
